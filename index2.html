Rooster Teeth

Rooster is a community office that does pod-casts on issues, or just on funny topics. They also make merchandise and funny videos on every day life at Rooster Teeth. They have a sub-division called Achievement Hunter.

Achievement Hunter

Achievement Hunter (AH) is a sub division of Rooster Teeth. Geoff Ramsey is the boss of the other 5 Achievement Hunters. Jack Pattillo, Ryan Haywood, Michael Jones, Gavin Free, and Ray Narveas Jr. They play video games for a living and post it on youtube and the rooster teeth website.
